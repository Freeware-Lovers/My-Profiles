My Profiles

This should be a lightweight and attractive front page, which will encourage new users to register and store the links to there profiles here.

Potential destinations from here: [register], [login], [contact], [privacy], [terms]...

Upon registration, for each user a new profile will be creaated with their name, picture, short bio and links to their social media profiles.

The URL of an individual profile will be in the following format: http://myprofiles.net/{UUID}. UUID = universally unique identifier, a 32 characters long unique string like e.g. 123e4567e89b12d3a456426614174000.

Alternatively, we might come up with our own format of unique strings, which will be shorter (~10-12 characters?) and exclude ambigous characters to minimize misinterpretations.
